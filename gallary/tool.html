LOCAL INFORMATION

Whether you're in Nairobi for business or pleasure, the Villa Rosa Kempinski is ideally situated for you to make the most out of the Kenyan capital. We're only 20km away from the airport, meaning that an approximate 30-minute drive is all that separates you from us when you land in Kenya. You'll find us on Chiromo Road, right at the heart of the commercial centre of Nairobi - just a hop, skip and a jump away from everything the area has to offer. Please get in touch today with any booking or event inquires - our expert team of staff are on hand to ensure that your visit to Villa Rosa is the most incredible that it can be.

Arrival Information

The Villa Rosa Kempinski is located adjacent to the Westlands area of Nairobi, an affluent neighbourhood in the city. The Westlands area is characterized by a cluster of shops, restaurants and night clubs.

Our Concierge Team will be delighted to arrange for your transportation with the Hotel Limousine or a Hotel Taxi.

The cost of a one way transfer using the Hotel Limousine is USD 80, and the cost of a one way transfer using the Hotel Taxi is USD 50. 

Please contact us 12 hours prior to your flight arrival time to arrange for a hotel limousine or hotel taxi.

Please contact us on (20) 360 3000 and request for the Concierge desk to book a hotel taxi or the hotel limousine. You could alternatively e mail us at concierge.villarosa@kempinski.com.

If you would like to hire a vehicle for self drive on rental basis, our concierge team can make the arrangements so that you get the vehicle at the airport. For this service we would need 48 hours notice prior to arrival. Please contact us on concierge.villarosa@kempinski.com to book this service.

Directions

The Villa Rosa Kempinski is located on the West side of the city, 3 km away from the central business district. The hotel is 20 Km West from Jomo Kenyatta International Airport.

Join Mombasa Road and drive West towards the City Center, 15 Km into the drive, you will pass the Nyayo National Stadium to your left. 

Drive on straight to join Waiyaki Way and at a 1 km distance drive from the University Way Roundabout you will find a U - Turn to your right, take the turn and drive as though you are heading back towards the City Center.

If there is no traffic on the roads, approximate travel time from the International Airport, Jomo Kenyatta International airport to the hotel is thirty minutes and during rush hour the travel time can be from one hour and thirty minutes
